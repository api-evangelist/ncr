NCR Voyix Corporation, a platform-powered leader in unified commerce for shopping and dining, today announced the commencement of the previously disclosed transition of its self-checkout and point-of-sale hardware businesses to an outsourced original design and manufacturing model through its commercial agreement with Ennoconn Corporation, a global technology solutions provider delivering world-class Industrial intelligent IoT, AI and Embedded Technology solutions.
