Repsol has taken a major step toward the future of service station experiences with the launch of Spain’s first station equipped with an innovative self-checkout kiosk. This cutting-edge solution allows customers to make payments completely autonomously, significantly enhancing efficiency and convenience.With this new technology, users can pay for both fuel and in-store products simply by scanning items and selecting their preferred payment method. The kiosk supports multiple payment options, including credit cards and the Waylet app, ensuring a fast, secure, and seamless shopping experience.
